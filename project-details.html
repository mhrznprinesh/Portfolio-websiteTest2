Municipal Transport Master Plan

Comprehensive transport planning for sustainable urban development

Client: Suryabinayak Municipality, Surunga Municipality, Pathari Shanishchare Municipality, Dharmadevi Municipality Year: 2022-2023 Category: Urban Planning Role: GIS Specialist

Project Overview

The Municipal Transport Master Plan project involved developing comprehensive transport strategies for multiple municipalities in Nepal, including Suryabinayak Municipality, Pathari Shanishchare Municipality, Surunga Municipality, and Dharmadevi Municipality. The goal was to create sustainable, efficient, and future-ready transport systems that would serve the growing urban populations.

As part of the consulting team, I was responsible for conducting road inventory surveys, spatial analysis using GIS tools, and preparing thematic maps that formed the basis for the transport planning decisions.

Challenges & Solutions

The project faced several challenges including limited existing data, rapidly changing urban landscapes, and the need to balance development with environmental concerns. Our team addressed these through:

Data Collection

Conducted extensive field surveys using DGPS and drone technology to gather accurate spatial data where existing records were insufficient.

Stakeholder Engagement

Organized ward-level consultations to understand local needs and incorporate community feedback into planning.

Sustainable Design

Prioritized non-motorized transport infrastructure and green corridors to reduce environmental impact.

Technology Integration

Used advanced GIS analysis to model traffic flows and identify optimal routes and infrastructure needs.

Project Outcomes

The completed Municipal Transport Master Plans provided each municipality with:

  • A 10-year roadmap for transport infrastructure development
  • Detailed GIS databases of existing road networks and conditions
  • Prioritized lists of infrastructure projects with cost estimates
  • Policy recommendations for sustainable transport management
  • Public engagement strategies for implementation
